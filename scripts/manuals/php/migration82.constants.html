  • TCP_KEEPALIVE (MacOS)
  • TCP_KEEPCNT (Linux, others)
  • TCP_KEEPIDLE (Linux, others)
  • TCP_KEEPINTVL (Linux, others)
  • TCP_NOTSENT_LOWAT
  • LOCAL_CREDS_PERSISTENT (FreeBSD)

  • LOCAL_CREDS (NetBSD)
  • SO_BPF_EXTENSIONS
  • SO_SETFIB

  • SO_ZEROCOPY (Linux)
  • MSG_ZEROCOPY (Linux)
